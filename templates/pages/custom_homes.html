Custom Homes & Design–Build

From first ideas to final walkthrough, Heritage can help you design and build a home that fits your life, your lot, and your budget.

How the Process Works

  1. Initial Conversation. Talk through timing, budget, and what you’re looking for.
  2. Homesite & Community. Pick a lot in one of our communities or discuss building on your land.
  3. Plan & Options. Choose a floor plan or start from a custom design, then refine layout and finishes.
  4. Construction. We handle permitting, scheduling, and build management.
  5. Closing & Move-In. Final walkthrough, punch list, and keys.
